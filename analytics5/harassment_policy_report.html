7. Recommendations

  • Mandatory Training: Formal training should be instituted across departments to improve awareness and reporting clarity.
  • Clarify Reporting Channels: Low awareness of whom to report to indicates a need for clearer communication protocols.
  • Target Gender Disparities: Gender-based differences in awareness require tailored outreach.
  • Continuous Learning: Refresher programs should be scheduled periodically.
  • Accessible Policy Documents: Ensure policies are visible and easy to locate on internal platforms.
